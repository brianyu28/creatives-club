Creatives Club DC

A group for creatives of all types

Creatives Club DC is the original Creatives Club! CCDC has a free, open meetup every Thursday from 6 - 9 pm. In the spring, summer, and fall, the location alternates between The Labs at the MLK Library (DCPL Central Library), and Temperance Alley Garden. In the winter, most meetups happen in at The Labs, with some happening in the upstairs meeting rooms of the MLK Library. Meetup locations are subject to change, so please check the Instagram page or the Discord server for the most up-to-date info!

Join our Discord server!

Our Discord server is the main way our community stays in contact! We have topic-based channels for different conversations, including places specifically for sharing your art, learning about events in the area, and organzing subgroups!

Follow us on Instagram!

On our Instagram page, we share updates about our meetups and promote local artists: @CreativesClubDC
