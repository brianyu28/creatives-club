Welcome to
Creatives Club!

A group for creatives of all types

Creatives Club provides a space for creatives to meet other like-minded folks and socialize while working on projects. Typically people bring their own things to work on, but there are always people looking for others to collaborate with!

Creatives Club started in Washington, DC, but now there are also groups in Boulder and Denver, Colorado as well as Durham, North Carolina!

Join our Discord server!

Our Discord server is the main way our community keeps in touch! If you've never used Discord before, think of it like a big group chat split into different channels like Slack or Microsoft Teams.

The server unites all the clubs in one virtual space, although there are parts of the server that are region-specific in addition to topic-based channels for different conversations. We have places specifically for sharing your art, learning about events in your area, and organzing subgroups!
